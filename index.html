About

Operations is the bridge between strategy and execution. I've spent 15 years building that bridge — for founders, CEOs and leadership teams who need senior operational firepower without the full-time hire.

I lead business operations and performance for fast-moving companies — designing the OKR and KPI frameworks that turn ambiguity into measurable progress, the financial structure that supports fundraising, and the cross-functional rhythm that lets a team actually ship. My work has spanned ad tech, mobile gaming, e-commerce, foodtech, supply-chain SaaS and media, with deep exposure across The Walt Disney Company, Gameloft, Talabat, Groupon, Vodafone, Audiomob and others.

Today I work as a fractional Head of Operations and partner directly with founders — and I'm orienting toward Chief of Staff work, where my background sitting alongside CEOs, CTOs and boards translates most naturally. I'm most useful in the messy middle, where the model is working but the company is outgrowing its early scaffolding.
